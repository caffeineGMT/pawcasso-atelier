# Day 2 — Cat with a Pearl Earring

## Caption

Vermeer never had a subject this composed.

Three centuries after Johannes painted his luminous girl, we present the definitive reinterpretation — a feline of impeccable poise, draped in ultramarine and gold, the famous pearl catching light precisely as the Dutch master intended. The craquelure tells you this painting has lived. The expression tells you she's unimpressed.

Some art historians will argue. The cat does not care.
