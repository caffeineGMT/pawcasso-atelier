# Day 1 — Lion Portrait

## Caption

A study in sovereign light.

His mane catches fire where shadow meets gold — each strand rendered with the patience of a Baroque master tracing candlelight across velvet. There is a stillness here that recalls Rembrandt's self-portraits: a subject who knows exactly who he is, and needs no one to confirm it.

The warm sienna deepens beneath his jaw. The amber holds in his gaze. This is not a portrait of a lion. This is a portrait of authority.
