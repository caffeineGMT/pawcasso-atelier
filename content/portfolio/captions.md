# Pawcasso Atelier -- Portfolio Captions

## Classic Portrait Series

### border_collie_renaissance.png
**Title:** The Scholar's Gaze
A study in devotion and Renaissance grace. Meet Callum -- a Border Collie whose luminous eyes hold centuries of wisdom, captured in golden chiaroscuro worthy of the old masters. Intelligence has never looked so noble.

### owl_art_nouveau.png
**Title:** The Keeper of Pages
Perched upon volumes of forgotten lore, this magnificent owl emerges from a world of flowing lines and jewel-toned splendor. An Art Nouveau tribute to the quiet guardians of knowledge and the night.

### fox_impressionist.png
**Title:** Winter's Warmth
A fleeting moment in a snow-dusted birch forest -- a fox pauses, impossibly warm against the cool blues of winter. Painted in the tender brushwork of the Impressionists, this piece reminds us that beauty lives in stillness.

### persian_cat_baroque.png
**Title:** Velvet & Vanity
Draped in crimson and bathed in candlelight, this Persian cat commands the room with an air of effortless opulence. A Baroque masterwork for the most discerning of feline connoisseurs.

## Pop Culture & Fantasy Series

### corgi_throne.png
**Title:** The Short King
Every kingdom needs a ruler with heart -- and short legs. This corgi ascends the iron throne with a crown of gold and an expression that says, "I shall rule with belly rubs and justice."

### penguin_gala.png
**Title:** Black Tie Affair
The most dapper guest at this evening's gala needs no introduction. Impeccably dressed (as always), champagne in flipper, he moves through the ballroom with the quiet confidence of one born in a tuxedo.

### red_panda_bookshop.png
**Title:** Chapter & Verse
Rain taps softly on the windows as lantern light spills across towers of well-loved books. Behind the counter, a bespectacled red panda tends to the shop with the gentle care of someone who believes every story matters.

### wolf_ukiyoe.png
**Title:** Song of the Mountain
Beneath a moon so full it seems to breathe, a lone wolf lifts its voice to the heavens. Rendered in the timeless tradition of the Japanese woodblock, this piece captures the raw poetry of the wild.
