# Day 3 — Hamster Ramen Shop

## Caption

Irasshaimase! One bowl, extra tiny.

Down a lantern-lit alley in a rain-soaked corner of Tokyo, the smallest chef in the city ladles broth with the quiet dedication of a lifelong craftsman. Steam curls into the neon haze. Raindrops tap the awning like applause. Every detail — the handwritten menu, the careful arrangement of toppings, the warm glow against wet cobblestone — drawn with the tenderness Miyazaki brought to every frame.

Some restaurants earn their stars. This one earns its whiskers.
